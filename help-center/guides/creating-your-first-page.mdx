## Overview

Create and publish content easily in En.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Log in, select your project from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new page">
    Click "New Page" and give it a title.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write content">
    Use the markdown editor for text, code, and images.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Hit "Publish" to make your page visible.
  </Step>
</Steps>
