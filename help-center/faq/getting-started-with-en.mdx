## Overview

En makes it easy to manage all your documentation in one place.

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://en.io` and log in.
2. Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
3. Name your project and choose a visibility option.
4. Start adding pages with the editor.
5. Organize your docs with folders as needed.
