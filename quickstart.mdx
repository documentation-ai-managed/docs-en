## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for version control integration
</Callout>

## Create Your En Account

Follow these steps to sign up and verify your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit En Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to the [En dashboard](https://app.en.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link from `no-reply@en.com` and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer the quick setup questions about your project type to personalize your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Default Documentation Space

Once logged in, create your first space to organize project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="folder-plus">
    Click **New Space** in the sidebar and name it `My Project Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space Settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to **Private** or **Public**, add a description like "Documentation for my En project", and choose the default template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Collaborators" icon="users">
    Optionally, share the space invite link with team members via email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Document

Create a document and add content using MDX.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document** and title it "Introduction".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit in MDX" icon="edit-3">
    Switch to the MDX editor and add frontmatter and content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Preview changes in real-time, then click **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX Example">
  ```mdx
  ---
  title: Introduction
  description: Welcome to the project.
  ---

  ## Overview

  This is your first En document.
  ```
</CodeGroup>

## Interface Tour

Explore key areas of the En interface.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Sidebar Navigation" default-open="true">
    The left sidebar shows your spaces, documents, and search. Use it to switch contexts quickly.

    - **Spaces**: List of documentation spaces
    - **Recent Docs**: Quick access to edited files
    - **Search**: Global search across all content
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Editor Toolbar">
    Top toolbar includes preview toggle, publish button, and version history.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Preview Pane">
    Real-time rendering of MDX with components like `<Callout>` and code blocks.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
